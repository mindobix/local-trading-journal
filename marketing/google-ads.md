# Google Ads — Local Trading Journal
> Responsive Search Ads (RSA) targeting traders paying for SaaS journal subscriptions

---

## Campaign Structure

**Campaign Goal:** Conversions (GitHub page visits / app downloads)
**Campaign Type:** Search
**Bidding:** Maximize Clicks (start), then Target CPA once data accumulates
**Daily Budget Suggestion:** $15–30/day to start

## Suggested Ad Schedule

Best times to reach active traders:
- **Weekdays 6:00 AM – 10:00 AM** (pre-market research)
- **Weekdays 3:30 PM – 6:00 PM** (post-market review)
- **Sunday 6:00 PM – 9:00 PM** (week prep)

Reduce bids by 30–50% on weekends midday and overnight hours.

---

## A/B Test Priority

1. **Headline 2 vs 4** — Does "Stop Paying $50/Mo" or "No Subscription. Ever." drive more clicks?
2. **Pain-led ("Cancel TraderSync")** vs **benefit-led ("Track R-Multiple Per Trade")**
3. **Privacy angle** vs **cost angle** — which resonates more with your audience?

Run each test for minimum 300 impressions per variant before drawing conclusions.
